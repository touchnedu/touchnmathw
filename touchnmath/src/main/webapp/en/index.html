about

TOUCHNMATH is a must-have math study application; No need to have math books, study books, school supplies or desk, only with Smartphone you can get familiar with all mathematical concepts, study and solves math problems all by yourself.

Studying math is a process of getting acquainted with mathematical concepts, learning how to solve related exercises and practicing many times so that they can be ingrained in your head.

When a baby starts walking, it takes hundreds of thousands times of falling to practice; and it also takes tens or hundreds times of practice to finally know that 1 + 2 = 3 and then 3 + 6 = 9. But many students just study math with eyes in which they only calculate some single digit additions and practice cursorily as if children can already stand up and walk after several falls.


You need to spend time to re-solve the math problems that you had already put time and effort to sit on the table and hold the pen to learn. However, many students tend to find excuses and give up halfway.

We have been thinking a lot about how to make students who had given up to pick up the pen and start studying again. TOUCHNMATH has been created to serve that purpose. Students now can do all the math problems with some simple touches whenever they have spare time. It will gradually turn into a pleasure and help students realize that they can do all the basic math in middle and high school, thus have the courage to continue learning math.
